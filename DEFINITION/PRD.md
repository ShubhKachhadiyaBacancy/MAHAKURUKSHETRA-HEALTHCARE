# SpecialtyRx Connect -- Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Product Overview

SpecialtyRx Connect is a healthcare platform designed to streamline the
process of managing specialty medications. It connects patients,
providers, pharmacies, and payers to simplify therapy initiation, prior
authorization, financial assistance, and treatment management.

## 2. Problem Statement

Specialty drug therapies face several challenges: - Complex prior
authorization processes - Insurance verification delays - High patient
out-of-pocket costs - Low medication adherence - Poor coordination
between providers, pharmacies, and payers

These issues cause therapy delays, administrative burden, and reduced
patient outcomes.

## 3. Goals & Objectives

Primary goals: - Simplify specialty medication onboarding - Reduce prior
authorization approval time - Improve patient adherence - Provide
real-time visibility for healthcare providers - Automate patient support
workflows

## 9. MVP Scope

Initial MVP will include: - Patient enrollment portal - Prior
authorization management - Insurance verification - Financial assistance
coordination - Provider dashboard - Patient communication system

## 10. Monetization Strategy

-   Per-patient enrollment fees
-   SaaS subscriptions for providers
-   Transaction fees for authorization approvals
-   Premium analytics packages
-   Integration fees
-   White-label licensing

## 11. Success Metrics

-   Patient enrollment conversion rate
-   Prior authorization approval rate
-   Time to therapy initiation
-   Medication adherence rate
-   Patient satisfaction (NPS)
-   Provider adoption rate

## 12. Go-To-Market Strategy

Start with pharmaceutical manufacturers as primary customers, then
expand to health systems and specialty pharmacies. Focus on high-cost
therapies such as oncology, immunology, and rare diseases.
